Welcome to the SAGE Lab

The Software Automation, Generation, and Engineering (SAGE) Lab is a software engineering research group in the Department of Computer Science at George Mason University.

The research group is currently led by Dr. Kevin Moran, and pursues academic and industrial research with the primary focus of creating useful automation for software developers which allows them to write code efficently and with fewer errors.
