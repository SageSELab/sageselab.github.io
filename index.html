    Welcome to the SAGE Research Lab

    The Software Automation, Generation, and Engineering (SAGE) Lab is a software engineering research group in the Department of Computer Science at the Universtiy of Central Florida.

    The research group is led by Dr. Kevin Moran, and pursues academic research with the primary focus of creating useful automation for software developers which allows them to write code efficently and accurately.
